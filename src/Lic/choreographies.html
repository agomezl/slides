| A<b> -> B; G(Sel)

$(s,c) \to (s',c')$

Where:

  • $c$ and $c'$ are choreographies
  • $s$ and $s'$ are mappings from pairs of process and variables to values

Communication:

$s(p,x)=d$$p \neq q$
$(s,p[x] \to q.y ; c) \xrightarrow{(\text{com p x q y},[])} (s[(q,y) \mapsto d], c)$

Selection:

$p \neq q$
$(s,p\langle b\rangle \to q.y ; c) \xrightarrow{(\text{Sel p b q},[])} (s, c)$

Branching:

$s(p,v)=\top$
$(s, \text{If}\,a@p\,\text{Then}\,c_1\,\text{Else}\,c_2) \xrightarrow{(\text{LTau p v},[])} (s, c_1)$
